Snowd'In

Snowd'In solved the problem of getting exagerated or innacurate snow reports. We scraped each of our local mountains for their daily snow cast and we allow our users to rate the report as 'Fresh' or 'False'. We also allowed users to connect with each others on the mountain via websockets that were associated with a mountain. Winner of the Lighthouse Labs October Cohort contest.
